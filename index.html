Our Creative Process

A collaborative journey from manuscript to ready-for-print illustration masterpieces.

01. Discovery

We analyze your manuscript or article brief to conceptualize metaphorical ideas that capture the central message.

02. Concept & Ink

Drafting composition layouts and refining the artwork using high-quality digital ink-wash and vector brushes.

03. Color & Print

Calibrating color styling for pre-print high-resolution CMYK or responsive web RGB layouts.
